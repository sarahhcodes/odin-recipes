Toast

Description

It's just toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Bread
  • Toppings (peanut butter, avocado, etc.)

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread and place in toaster.
  2. Select your desired level of browning. (This varies according to toaster, refer to your toaster's manual if you get stuck.)
  3. Press down on the toaster lever.
  4. Wait until toast pops up.
  5. Remove toast from toaster and top with your favorite toppings.*

*we recommend spreading melted butter across the surface of the toast. yum!
